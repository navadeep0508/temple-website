About Our Temple

Welcome to the Sri Kodandarama Swami Temple, located in the peaceful village of Ulavapalla, in Dagadarthi Mandal, SPSR Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh. This temple is a special place for those seeking peace and spiritual comfort. Surrounded by beautiful nature, it is a place where tradition and devotion come together. The temple is decorated with detailed sculptures, and the sounds of prayers fill the air. Visitors can take part in daily rituals and feel the calm, sacred energy of the temple.
